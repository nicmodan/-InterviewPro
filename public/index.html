How it works

Two roles, one seamless flow. Get up and running in minutes.

🛡️ For Admins
1

Create your account

Sign up with your email and password. Your admin dashboard is ready instantly.

2

Set up your form

Add exam options, set the application fee, and customise interview questions.

3

Share the link

Copy your unique form link and share it with applicants via any channel.

4

Track everything

Monitor applications, payments, and analytics from your dashboard in real time.

👤 For Applicants
1

Open the shared link

Click the link shared by the institution. No account needed — just open and go.

2

Fill the form

Enter personal details, address, and select your exam. Review before submitting.

3

Make payment

Pay the application fee securely via Monnify — card, transfer, or USSD.

4

Get confirmation

Receive instant confirmation of your application and payment on screen.
